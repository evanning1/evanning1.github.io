Getting Started

Step 1: Navigate to the settings button on your Gmail

Step 2: Click "See all settings"

Step 3: Click "Forwading and POP/IMAP"

Step 4: Enable IMAP

Step 5: Navigate to your google account page and click "Security"

Step 6: Click "2-Step Verification"

Step 7: Scroll down and click "App passwords"

Step 8: Create a username for a new app password. This will be used later

Step 9: Google will give you a password. Save this password for later
